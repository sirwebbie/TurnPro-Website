Conceived by a Conservation Officer, many years of development have gone into making this Patented Product a must have where there is any chance of a drowning due to a swimming or fall through the ice accident. Every year, thousands of Americans die in ponds and lakes due to the absence of a potential life saving rescue apparatus.

Each Lifeline Rescue Station includes:

  • Lifeline Rescue Bag and Harness
  • Mounting Bolts
  • Security Seals

Each year lives are tragically lost due to water and ice related accidents. When tragedy strikes, immediate action is needed to help save the life of a child or neighbor from drowning or hypothermia. LIFELINE is the first Outdoor Rescue Station designed to be ready 24 hours a day to help in the event of a water or ice emergency.

Each LIFELINE Rescue Station consists of a LDPE Molded Base structure, an 80 foot Lifeline Rescue Bag and Harness, safety seals and mounting instructions. (Requires mounting on a standard 4x4 post. Post is not included.)

Each LIFELINE Rescue Bag can be reused again and again. The rope is simply fed back into the bag in a hand over hand fashion and the harness is pulled back through from the bottom deployment opening prior to restuffing the rope.
